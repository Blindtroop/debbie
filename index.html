Makeup tutorial

Hi there! My name is Debby, and I'm just about to apply my makeup for the day. I start by moisturizing my face to make sure my skin is nice and smooth. Next, I apply a primer to help my makeup last all day. I then use a beauty blender to apply my foundation, making sure to blend it in well. After that, I move onto my eyes, using eyeshadow to create a natural look, followed by eyeliner and mascara to make my eyes pop. I finish off with some blush on my cheeks and a swipe of lipstick. Voila! I'm ready to take on the day.

Today, I'm feeling inspired to go for a bold look, so I'm starting with a bright red lipstick. After applying a lip balm to prep my lips, I carefully apply the lipstick with a lip brush, making sure to stay within my lip line. Next, I'm using a smoky eye shadow palette to create a dramatic eye look. I blend the shadows together, making sure there are no harsh lines. I then apply eyeliner to the top lash line, followed by some mascara to complete the look. Finally, I add some highlighter to my cheekbones and finish off with a light dusting of setting powder. I feel like a new person with this bold makeup look, and I'm excited to see how it looks throughout the day.
